Fish cakes Recipe

The recipe is very easy and can be done by anyone even without cooking experiece

Follow the recipe to the t and you will not go wrong

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Hake
  • Cheddar cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Flour
  • Breadcrumbs

Method

  1. Boil Potato as per packet instruction and set aside
  2. Mash the potato and add the cheese, fish, sald and pepper
  3. Dip in flour, dip in egg then cover with breadcrumbs
  4. Air fry for 10 minutes
