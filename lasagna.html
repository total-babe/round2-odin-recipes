Lasagna Recipe

The recipe is very easy and can be done by anyone even without cooking experiece

Follow the recipe to the t and you will not go wrong

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ground beef mince
  • Onions
  • Cheese sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Cream

Method

  1. Boil Pasta as per packet instruction and set aside
  2. In a pan, saute onions and add beef
  3. Fry for 5 minutes
  4. Add cream and pasta, then simmer on low heat
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Add to baking dish, pour cheese sauce on top, then bake for 10 minutes on 180 degrees
